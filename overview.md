IRIS CARBON is a cloud-based disclosure management platform designed to help organizations prepare, validate, and file structured financial and regulatory reports.

The platform enables enterprises to create, manage, and collaborate on reporting documents (such as financial statements, ESG disclosures, and regulatory filings) while ensuring compliance with global mandates like XBRL, iXBRL, SEC, ESMA, HMRC, and others.

CARBON operates on a document-centric architecture where each report exists as a version-controlled document. All data, content, and validations are tied to a specific document version, ensuring traceability, auditability, and consistency.

Key capabilities include:
- Structured report creation and editing
- XBRL/iXBRL tagging and taxonomy mapping
- Validation of financial data (totals, subtotals, consistency checks)
- Multi-user collaboration and workflow management
- Integration with external systems for data ingestion and submission

CARBON also incorporates an AI layer (IRA – IRIS AI Assistant) that enhances productivity by:
- Generating and summarizing disclosure content
- Answering queries based on document context
- Detecting anomalies in financial data
- Producing validation and analysis reports

All AI-driven outputs are strictly scoped to the selected document version to ensure accuracy, reliability, and compliance.

The platform is built to support enterprise-scale reporting, reduce manual effort, improve data accuracy, and streamline the end-to-end disclosure process from preparation to submission.
